Last Summer, I camped in the mountains with my parents. I saw a beautiful yellow butterfly while my parents were cooking dinner next to the tent. I followed it and wanted to catch it. After I walked for a long time, I found I got lost. I felt very nervous, so I began to run back quickly. Suddenly, I fell over.

“What should I do?” I cried. When I looked around, I saw a man and a dog walking towards me.

“Your mom and dad called Search and Rescue(搜救队) for help, because they couldn't find you anywhere. Are you feeling well?” the man asked.

“Oh, no, my left foot hurts and I can't walk.”

“Don't worry. I will call 120. The 

ambulance

 will arrive here soon.” said the man.

“How did you find me?” I asked in surprise.

“I let my dog Bella smell your jacket. She followed your smell and found you.” he said

The ambulance arrived and took me to the hospital. When we got to the hospital, mom and dad were waiting there. I had an X-ray of my left foot. Luckily, it wasn't broken. But I needed to stay in bed for several days. Mom and dad thanked everyone who helped me. And we thanked Bella, too.
